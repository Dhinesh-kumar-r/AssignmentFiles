NEWS OF THE DAY

iB cricket announces world's first
Virtual reality cricket League

iB Cricket is a virtual reality (VR) cricket game that launched in the presence of the then Hon'ble President Shri Ram Nath Kovind. The game is available on Steam and the Oculus Store. iB Cricket announced the Metaverse Super Star, the world's first VR cricket league. The league includes 12 international cricketers from India, Australia, New Zealand, and Sri Lanka.
